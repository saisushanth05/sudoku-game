How to Play

Fill the Sudoku board by clicking an empty cell and selecting a number from the floating buttons below.

  • Each row, column, and 3x3 box must contain numbers from 1 to 9 without repetition.
  • Complete the board before the timer ends to win the level.
  • If time runs out, you'll start from Level 1.
